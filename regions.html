Explore Bangladesh

Adventure Regions Across the Country

North Bengal

Highlights: Hill treks, tea gardens, bamboo forests

Explore Sylhet's tea estates, Jaintia Hills, and hidden waterfalls. Perfect for trekking and nature photography.

Popular trips: Jaflong Trek, Ratargul Swamp Expedition

Southeast Coast

Highlights: Sandy beaches, coral islands, water activities

Cox's Bazar, St. Martin's Island, and Teknaf offer pristine beaches and island exploration. Ideal for coastal camps.

Popular trips: Teknaf Beach Camp, St. Martin's Island Tour

Sundarbans Region

Highlights: Mangroves, river routes, wildlife viewing

The world's largest mangrove forest offers eco-cruises, boat expeditions, and unique ecosystem exploration.

Popular trips: Sundarbans Eco-Cruise, Tiger Reserve Journey

Central Bangladesh

Highlights: River systems, pastoral villages, cultural sites

Navigate the Meghna and Padma rivers, explore village life, and discover ancient historical monuments.

Popular trips: Padma River Navigation, Rural Village Tours

Northwest Region

Highlights: Ancient ruins, agricultural lands, seasonal wetlands

Bogra, Naogaon, and Rajshahi offer historical exploration and seasonal bird-watching adventures.

Popular trips: Historical Route Trek, Seasonal Wetland Safari

Eastern Hills

Highlights: Mountain terrain, tribal villages, scenic landscapes

Chittagong Hill Tracts present challenging treks, cultural encounters, and pristine natural beauty.

Popular trips: Hill Tract Trek, Kaptai Lake Expedition
